1. Strengthen Basic Arithmetic and Algebra

  • Topics to Cover:

    • Understanding polynomials and factoring.
    • Simplifying expressions.

  • Resources:

2. Dive Deeper into Geometry and Trigonometry

  • Why? You need a solid understanding of shapes, angles, and trigonometric functions for calculus and linear algebra.

  • Topics to Cover:

    • Geometry: Properties of triangles, circles, and polygons.
    • Trigonometry: Sine, cosine, tangent, unit circle, and trigonometric identities.
    • Graphs of trigonometric functions.

  • Resources:

    • Book: Geometry for Enjoyment and Challenge by Richard Rhoad (accessible and structured).
    • Free Course: Khan Academy - Geometry and Trigonometry.

3. Learn Pre-Calculus Concepts

  • Why? Pre-calculus bridges the gap between basic algebra/trigonometry and calculus.

  • Topics to Cover:

    • Functions: Domain, range, transformations, and inverses.
    • Exponential and logarithmic functions.
    • Sequences and series.
    • Intro to limits.

  • Resources:

    • Book: Precalculus: Mathematics for Calculus by James Stewart et al. (clear explanations).
    • Free Course: Khan Academy - Precalculus.

5. Explore Linear Algebra

  • Topics to Cover:

    • Matrix operations and properties.

  • Resources:

6. Begin Exploring Real Analysis

  • Why? Real analysis provides the rigorous foundation for calculus.

  • Topics to Cover:

    • Proofs in mathematics.
    • Limits and sequences in depth.
    • Continuity, differentiation, and integration.

  • Resources:
